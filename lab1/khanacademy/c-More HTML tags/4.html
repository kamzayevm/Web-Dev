Pasta Carbonara Chocolate Chip Cookies

Pasta Carbonara

Ingredients Amount
Spaghetti 200g
Bacon 150g
Eggs 2
Grated Parmesan Cheese 50g
Black Pepper To taste

Details

This classic Italian dish is quick and delicious. Make sure to use high-quality Parmesan cheese for the best flavor.

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Cook bacon until crispy, then chop into small pieces.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain cooked spaghetti and mix with the egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Add chopped bacon and season with black pepper. Toss until well combined.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients Amount
All-purpose Flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking Soda 1/2 teaspoon
Butter, softened 1 cup
Granulated Sugar 3/4 cup
Brown Sugar, packed 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Eggs 2
Chocolate Chips 2 cups

Details

These chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and full of chocolatey goodness. Perfect for any occasion!

Steps

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a small bowl, combine flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  • In a large bowl, cream together softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  • Beat in vanilla extract and eggs.
  • Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing well.
  • Stir in chocolate chips.
  • Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  • Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire racks.
  • Enjoy these delicious homemade chocolate chip cookies!
